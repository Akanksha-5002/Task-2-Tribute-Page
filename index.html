TIMELINE

1922-1950
  • Dec 28, 1922 - Stan Lee is born
  • May 3, 1939 - Took a job at Timely Comics Inc at age 17
  • May 1941-Made his comic-book debut with the text filler "Captain America Foils the Traitor's Revenge"
1950-1966
  • March 1950- Appears on the cover of Black Rider
  • Nov 6, 1961 - Creates The Fantastic Four
  • May 3, 1962 - Spider-Man is born
  • January 1963 - Appears in The Fantastic Four #10 as himself
  • March 1966 - Galactus Trilogy, frequently cited as Lee and Kirby's finest achievement was published
1969-1989
THE MARVEL AGE
  • 1969 - Created comics' first African-American superhero, the Falcon
  • 1972 - Assumed the role of publisher
  • 1980 -The Marvel Age:Made heroes like Doctor Strange, The Hulk, Iron Man, Thor and more over the last 20 years
2000
  • 1900s - Stepped away from regular duties at Marvel
    • 1900s - Stepped away from regular duties at Marvel
  • 2001- Formed POW! (Purveyors of Wonder) Entertainment along with Gill Champion, and Arthur Lieberman to develop film, television and video game properties.
2002-2006
  • 2002-Sued Marvel claiming that the company was failing to pay his share of the profits from movies featuring the characters he had created
  • 2006 - Marvel commemorated Lee's 65 years with the company by publishing a series of one-shot comics starring Lee himself meeting and interacting with many of his co-creations
2010-2018
  • September 2010 - The Stan Lee Foundation was founded. It's aim was to focus on literacy, education, and the arts
  • September 2012 - Lee underwent an operation to insert a pacemaker, which required cancelling planned appearances at conventions
  • November 12, 2018- Lee died, at the age of 95
